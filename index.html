Biography

Ahikyirize Daniel, was born in 2001 in the western side of Uganda. Being born from an average background, the situation forced him to mature up at a such a young age.
At 18 years, after college, he learned about forex trading from the internet, he picked up interest and decided to master the art. After trying out multiple online business like drop shipping, amazon sales and many more, I discovered day trading, I didn’t know about forex, stocks and cryptos, Daniel narrated.
Took some good time to master the art but it was definitely worth it. After mastering how to trade, Daniel dropped out of university in his first year and used tuition as the trading capital, “this was the biggest risk I had ever taken”, Daniel quoted.
Forex bulls was formed to serve the community, sharing trading ideas and helping people become profitable in the currency markets, its vital to provide value in a section you specialize in and get people achieve goals.It makes me feel proud when I can help someone achieve a goal through me, Ahikyirize said.
